NSS

The National Service Scheme (NSS) is a flagship initiative of the Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports, Government of India. It aims to cultivate a sense of social responsibility, civic awareness, and community engagement among the youth.

Open to students of higher secondary schools, colleges, universities, and technical institutions, NSS provides a dynamic platform for young minds to participate in community service, nation-building activities, and developmental programs driven by both government and grassroots efforts.

Rooted in the motto "NOT ME BUT YOU", the scheme inspires selfless action and fosters leadership, empathy, and a commitment to creating meaningful change in society.
